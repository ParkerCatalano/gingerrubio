Fine Purveyors of Urban Hair-apy

Ginger Rubio, located at 600 Shotwell Street in San Francisco's Mission District is a premiere hair salon, catering to all.  Hailing from some of the top salons in the city, Wendy Kirsten and Anthony Lymon joined forces to create a salon that focuses on the individual, their lifestyle and customer service. The salon is a sunny studio filled with laughter and good vibes, as the stylists are practically family.

Over time, they garnered other top stylists and are now considered a 'best kept secret' by those in the know. Color, cut, and extension experts, they stay on top of, and create, the trends in hairstyling.   A Bumble and Bumble salon, they only use the best to keep your new do look salon-spectacular.

HISTORY

Ginger Rubio Salon opened at 20th and Shotwell st in 2008.  Under the leadership of Wendy Kirsten and loveable  Mr. Anthony Lymon the salon evolved from a 3-chair affair and is now ramped up to 9 chairs and 10 stylists of every stripe catering to all.  Within these past years, the salon has been educated by Bumble and Bumble, Vidal Sassoon, Label M, Lakme and Schwarzkopf.

Under the leadership of grand dame Wendy Kirsten and loveable sidekick Mr. Anthony Lymon the salon evolved from a 3-chair affair and is now ramped up to 9 chairs and 10 stylists of every stripe catering to all, but specializing in hair solutions for those who find predictable salon offerings too pedestrian.

The Ginger Rubio family has also hosted and participated in photo shoots, art gallery openings, fashion shows, Thread and private party events.   For 6 Years, clients know that the staff of Ginger Rubio will never leave them wanting.
