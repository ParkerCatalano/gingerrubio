Heather caruso

  • Q: How long have you been doing hair?

    A: 10 years

  • Q: Specialties?

    A: Styles and Updos, Special Occasion, Bridal Hair and Red Carpet. Cut and color, blondes, curly hair, consultations. I listen and work my hardest to give them what they want to achieve hair that’s pretty and healthy.

  • Q: Training?

    A: Schwarzkopf, Carlton Hair, B&B.

  • Q: Favorite Hair Era?

    A: Pin Curls 20’s 40’s and 50’s. Vintage inspired styles, runway stuff. Vintage waves, Ginger Rogers, retro.

  • Q: Musical Influences?

    A: Nick Cave, Radio Head, Explosions the Sky, Bon Iver, Dead Mouse, Pretty Lights.

  • Q: Style Influences?

    A: Mash up: 40’s and 70s, bohemian meets underground with a dash of naturalist. Tribal influences, but I like to keep it pretty.

  • Q: Why do you love working here?

    A: It’s a family, and anybody is accepted in. We are all here to make peoples day better, and to make people feel better about themselves from the inside out (or the outside in!). I make people look good on the outside so their true colors on the inside can shine.
