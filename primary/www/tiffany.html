Tiffany Barrios

  • Q: How long have you been doing hair?

    A: 10 years

  • Q: Specialties?

    A: Short cuts, Vidal Sassoon type cuts, now. Working with curly hair. Fun colors: interesting color palettes (ie: contrasts). Short hair needs to look like an intentional decision.

  • Q: Training?

    A: B&B Swartzkopf, Vidal Sasson, Sebastian, Goldwell, Rene Furtere

  • Q: Favorite Hair Era?

    A: Current, mixture of everything cool that has happened in the past, retranslated into now.

  • Q: Musical Influences?

    A: Club kid, hip hop, house, punk rock, classic rock, jazz.

  • Q: Style Influences?

    A: Yves San Laurent, Vivienne Westwood, McQueen, Richard Avendon and Vidal Sasson.

  • Q: Why do you love working here?

    A: It’s fun, the atmosphere is light. It’s my safe haven, I come here and everything melts away. I love the people I work with - they are so much fun, and I love what I do, so I’m happy to be able to do it in a place that I also love.
