Professional Services

We don't sit on our laurels, or just stay in our salon, snip and color all day. We thirst for knowledge: best practices, new techniques, formulas and trends to ensure that we are up to the minute on how to give clients our best.

We have a passion for hair and techniques. There are some, who after a long day at the salon would pass on the latest techniques class or a new line of color. But we beg to differ: the devil is in the details, and diving into the wonderful world of hair education brings clients the professional innovation they deserve.
