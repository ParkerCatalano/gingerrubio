  • About Kelle

    Kelle has been doing hair for 13 years and is still extremely passionate about her art. She has trained with Carlton Hair International, Vidal Sassoon, Toni and Guy, American Crew, Schwarzkopf and Bumble&Bumble.

    Kelle has a wide range of clientele, and loves the challenge of a makeover. Weather it's a sleek bob or curly shag, Kelle will rock your locks. Besides styling hair, Kelle loves her pets, biking, fashion, arts and crafts, Johnny Depp and photography."
